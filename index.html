Column Guide — What Everything Means

🟢 Status Badge

⬆ STRONG (green)
Stock is a non-constituent ranked inside the add buffer (e.g. rank ≤179 for ASX 200). High probability of being added at next rebalance if price holds.
⬇ STRONG (red)
Current constituent has dropped outside the deletion buffer (e.g. rank ≥221 for ASX 200). High probability of removal.
◆ POSS (purple)
Within ~25 ranks of the buffer. Could move in or out. Watch closely — especially if price is trending.
○ WATCH (grey)
Within ~50 ranks of the buffer. On the radar, needs a meaningful move to cross.

📊 Market Cap and Rank

FF MKT CAP
Float-Adjusted Market Cap — the ranking metric S&P uses.
Price × Shares × IWF
S&P uses the 3-month daily average. This tool uses the live spot value as a real-time proxy. They will be close but not identical.
Example: A$2.5b = float-adjusted market cap of $2.5 billion
RANK
Current position ranked by FF Mkt Cap. Rank 1 = largest. The bar shows position vs the buffer threshold.
Green = inside buffer · Amber = near buffer · Red = outside buffer
IWF (Investable Weight Factor)
Free float fraction. IWF 1.0 = all shares publicly tradeable. IWF 0.6 = 40% held by insiders.
Min IWF of 0.15 required for index entry.
Warning: For dual-listed stocks (BHP, RIO, AMC), Yahoo uses global share count — ASX CDI count is far lower, making FMC appear larger than the ASX reality.

💧 Price and Liquidity

LIVE PRICE
Current price from Yahoo Finance (15-20 min delayed). The % shows today's move. As price moves, FF Mkt Cap and rank update every 60 seconds.
RL% (Relative Liquidity)
Hard eligibility gate — failing disqualifies a stock regardless of rank.
(MDVT / FF Mkt Cap) / market average × 100
≥50% = eligible for ASX 200 and above
≥30% = eligible for ASX 300
25-50% = at risk of removal from ASX 200
<25% = below removal threshold, will be removed
Note: on-market volume only — actual RL slightly higher due to off-market trades.
MDVT
Median Daily Value Traded in A$m/day over 3 months. Typical daily turnover in dollar terms.
Example: MDVT A$8.5m = on a typical day, $8.5 million of this stock changes hands on ASX.

💰 Passive Flow

FLOW A$m
Estimated passive fund buying or selling if this stock enters or exits the index.
(Stock FF Mkt Cap / Total Index FF Mkt Cap) × Passive AUM
+A$24m = passive funds need to BUY $24m at effective date
-A$657m = passive funds need to SELL $657m
Adjust AUM assumptions top-right. Defaults: ASX 200 = A$80bn, ASX 300 = A$15bn.
DTC (Days to Close)
Passive flow divided by daily volume. How many days of trading the passive flow represents.
DTC <1 = small impact, easily absorbed
DTC 1-5 = moderate, funds spread trading over a few days
DTC >5 = large relative to volume. Active managers can trade ahead of this.
Example: DTC 53d = passive funds need 53 days of avg volume to complete their trade.

🎯 Price to Include / Safety

PRICE TO INCLUDE
The average price this stock needs to sustain for the remaining reference period days to rank inside the add buffer by the reference date. This is NOT a one-day target — it is a sustained average.
-5% from spot = already qualifying
+12% from spot = needs 12% sustained rally
+45% from spot = very unlikely at this stage
PRICE TO SAFETY
For removal candidates: the average price the stock needs to recover to in order to rank safely inside the index. Shown as a price and % move needed from current spot.

📅 Key Dates and Rules

Reference Period
The 3-month window S&P uses to calculate average FMC and liquidity. The Reference Date (end of period) is when S&P takes the snapshot that determines index membership.
June 2026: period runs ~16 Feb to 22 May 2026
Announcement vs Effective
Announcement = 1st Friday of rebalance month. S&P publishes all changes. Stocks typically move on this day.
Effective = 3rd Friday. Passive funds must complete all trades by close. The 2-week window between these dates is when the actual buying and selling happens.
Buffer Rules
ASX 20: add ≤14th, remove ≥26th
ASX 50: add ≤39th, remove ≥61st
ASX 100: add ≤84th, remove ≥116th
ASX 200: add ≤179th, remove ≥221st
ASX 300: add ≤274th, remove ≥326th
Index Committee can override when M&A creates a vacancy mid-quarter.
