Column Guide — What Everything Means

🟢 Status Badge

⬆ STRONG (green)
Non-constituent ranked inside the addition buffer. High probability of being added if price holds to reference date.

e.g. rank ≤179 for ASX 200 addition, rank ≤274 for ASX 300 addition
⬇ STRONG (red)
Current constituent ranked outside the deletion buffer. High probability of removal.

e.g. rank ≥221 for ASX 200 removal, rank ≥326 for ASX 300 removal
◆ POSS (purple)
Within ~25 ranks of the buffer threshold. Price is close — watch for moves.
○ WATCH (grey)
Within ~50 ranks. On radar but needs a more meaningful move.

📊 Market Cap and Rank

FF MKT CAP (Float-Adjusted)
The S&P ranking metric: Price × Shares × IWF
S&P uses the 3-month daily average. This snapshot uses spot prices — directionally accurate, slightly different from official S&P ranks. Update prices via ✏️ to recalculate.
RANK
Position by FF Mkt Cap across all 331 tracked stocks.
Rank 1 = largest company.
Bar colour: Green=inside buffer · Amber=near · Red=outside
IWF (Investable Weight Factor)
Free float fraction applied to market cap. 1.0 = all shares publicly tradeable.
Dual-listed stocks (BHP, RIO, AMC, RMD, LNW, 360 etc) have CDI-adjusted IWF pre-applied.

💧 Price and Liquidity

PRICE
Snapshot price as of 22 Apr 2026. Click ✏️ Update Prices to paste fresh prices — rankings recalculate instantly across all 331 stocks.
RL% (Relative Liquidity)
Hard eligibility gate — a stock failing this cannot be included regardless of market cap rank.
(MDVT / FF Mkt Cap) / market weighted avg × 100
≥50% = ASX 200 eligible
≥30% = ASX 300 eligible
25-50% = at risk (removal zone for ASX 200)
<25% = removal threshold
MDVT
Median Daily Value Traded A$m/day over 3 months. Used to calculate RL% and DTC (Days to Close).

💰 Passive Flow

FLOW A$m
Estimated passive/index fund buying or selling if the stock enters or exits the index.
(Stock FF Mkt Cap / Index total FF Mkt Cap) × Passive AUM
+A$24m = passive funds need to BUY
-A$657m = passive funds need to SELL

Adjust passive AUM assumptions top-right. Defaults: ASX 200 = A$80bn, ASX 300 = A$15bn.
DTC (Days to Close)
Passive flow ÷ MDVT. How many days of average trading volume the passive flow represents.
DTC <1 = low impact, easily absorbed
DTC 1-5 = moderate, funds spread over a few days
DTC >5 = high impact — active managers can trade ahead of the rebalance

🎯 Price to Include / Safety

PRICE TO INCLUDE
The average price this stock needs to sustain over the remaining reference period days to rank inside the add buffer at the reference date.

This is NOT a one-day target. S&P ranks by the 3-month average, so sustained movement matters more than a single-day spike.

-5% from spot = already tracking to qualify
+12% from spot = needs a sustained 12% rally
+40%+ from spot = very unlikely at this stage
PRICE TO SAFETY
For removal candidates: average price needed to recover to rank safely inside the deletion buffer before the reference date.

📅 Key Rules and Dates

Buffer Rules (S&P/ASX Jan 2026)
ASX 20: add if ranked ≤14, remove if ≥26
ASX 50: add if ranked ≤39, remove if ≥61
ASX 100: add if ranked ≤84, remove if ≥116
ASX 200: add if ranked ≤179, remove if ≥221

The Index Committee can override these when M&A creates a vacancy mid-quarter.
Reference Period and Dates
S&P calculates average FMC over ~3 months ending at the Reference Date (2nd-to-last Friday of the prior month).

Announcement = 1st Friday rebalance month — stocks react on this day.
Effective = 3rd Friday — passive funds complete all trades.
